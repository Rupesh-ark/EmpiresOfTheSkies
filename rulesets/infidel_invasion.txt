1. Infidel Host Counters (Brown)

These counters represent the forces of the Infidel Empire.

    Identification: The backs are solid brown with "Infidel Host" printed in white . The fronts feature a brown background with a sword icon and a number .

    Stats & Shields: Ground Hosts do not have printed Shield values; they absorb hits equal to their Sword Strength alone. The only counter in this pool with a Shield is the Infidel Fleet, denoted by a black circle.

    Effects: Drawn randomly to accumulate in the Infidel Empire . If an attack symbol is drawn, they invade Faithdom and players must combine forces to defeat their total Sword strength.

Exact Breakdown (12 Counters):

    2 -> 30 Swords (Features a up arrow icon behind the sword) 

    3 -> 20 Swords 

    3 -> 15 Swords 

    3 -> 10 Swords 

    1 -> Infidel Fleet: 15 Swords and 5 Shields (Features a balloon icon and a black circle for shields). Effect: Patrols airspace, engages in piracy against Trade Routes, and forces aerial combat.

2. Contingent Counters (Rebels / Grand Army)

These multi-purpose counters represent either internal uprisings or allied non-player armies.

    Identification: The backs feature a purple and orange checkered/striped pattern with the word "Contingent" . The fronts have a plain white background with a sword icon and a number .

    Stats & Shields: These counters do not have printed Shield values . They rely entirely on their Sword values for attack power and absorb hits equal to their printed Sword Strength.

    Effects: They serve two different roles depending on the phase. During a Rebellion Event, they act as the "Rebels" internal enemy force . During an Infidel Invasion, they act as the "Grand Army" forces representing non-player realms defending Faithdom.

Exact Breakdown (20 Counters):

    1 -> 15 Swords 

    5 -> 12 Swords 

    7 -> 10 Swords 

    7 -> 7 Swords
